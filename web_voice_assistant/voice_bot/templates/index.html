Ethics and Privacy Statement

At Emma, we prioritize and respect the privacy of our users. We are committed to ensuring that any interaction with our voice assistant is conducted in an ethical and responsible manner.

• Data Privacy

We collect and process the necessary data to provide our voice assistant servics effectively. However, we strictly adhere to data protection regulations and employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information. Rest assured that we will not share, sell, or misuse any user data beyond the scope of improving our voice assistant's performance.

• User Consent

We seek your consent before collecting any personal data, ensuring that you are fully aware of the information being collected and the purpose behind it. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, and we will promptly take action to respect your decision.

• Data Storage

We store user data securely and only retain it for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. When the data is no longer needed, it will be deleted securely and irreversibly.

• Transparency

We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and will provide you with clear information on what data is collected, how it is used, and how long it is retained. If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy while using our voice assistant, please reach out to us through the provided contact information.

• Continual Improvement

We constantly strive to improve our voice assistant's performance and functionality. This may include analyzing user interactions and feedback to enhance user experience, but always with the utmost respect for your privacy.

By using Emma, you agree to the terms outlined in this ethics and privacy statement.
